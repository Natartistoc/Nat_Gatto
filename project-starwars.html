The Project

Teaser fully produced from scratch. I recreated the entire board game in 3D and independently led the full production using advanced digital techniques. The project includes storyboarding, 3D animatic (layout), retopology, 3D modeling, PBR texturing, animation, 3D rendering, as well as video editing. The teaser was designed to function both as a promotional piece and as a tutorial, with animations strictly respecting the game rules to ensure a clear understanding of gameplay and objectives. Deliverables included optimized assets, animation sequences, and EXR/MP4 renders, with clean versioning and structured files for seamless studio integration.
