Rendering for Marketing Asset Optimization • Product Design • Promotional Visuals

The Project

For Jungle Speed | Limited Edition, I contributed to both the promotional visuals and the redesign of the game’s iconic totem. This project extended beyond visual marketing to include the creation of the physical object itself.

As shown in the video just above, the model presented is the initial version provided by production. The video at the top of this page shows my sculpted version, finalized for mass production with a significantly higher level of detail.

The same high-definition asset was then optimized for marketing purposes, enabling the creation of realistic PBR renders while preserving all key details. Below are selected visuals created for packaging, online presentation, and promotional materials.
