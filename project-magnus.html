MAGNUM OPUS

3D SCULPTING • 3D PRINTING • PROTOTYPING • METAL CASTING • MOLDING • 2D GRAPHICS • ILLUSTRATION

The Project

I designed the game's central element: a metal-cast medallion, developed from provided specifications and original concept art. The project combined 3D sculpting, 3D printing, traditional art, and molding expertise, covering the full pipeline from prototyping to metal factory production. I also created the card backs and token illustrations to visually align with the medallion, delivering both 3D design and 2D graphics.
