Son Goku

3D Sculpting • Collectible Design • 3D Printing Ready

The Project

Here is a collectible I created from scratch using ZBrush, based on my own concept art and my love for this character. Dragon Ball Z is one of my favorite childhood anime series, and I had a great time sculpting this piece in 2014; it is one of my older works. At the time, I remember struggling to create the cloud in 3D because only 2D references were available, and there were very few Son Goku merchandise items or collectibles sculpted back then. This was my very first artwork created as a collectible statue intended for 3D printing. Fan art and 3D sculpture based on the anime Dragon Ball Z.
