Illustration • Lighting • Visual Storytelling

THE PROJECT

This project is a stylized narrative illustration centered on visual storytelling and mood. The scene explores character expression, interaction, and hierarchy through composition, lighting, and color harmony. Particular attention was given to fur rendering, material definition (glass, plastic, foliage), depth of field, and atmospheric lighting to guide the viewer's eye toward the focal point.

The illustration combines a whimsical concept with semi-realistic rendering, emphasizing contrast between soft organic elements and a branded object, while maintaining a cohesive, painterly aesthetic.
