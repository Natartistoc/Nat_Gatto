Orc Warrior | Print

Concept Creation • Figurine Sculpting (1/6) • 3D Printing Preparation

The Project

This project focused on the complete pipeline of creating a 1/6 scale collectible figurine. Beyond the artistic sculpt in ZBrush, significant attention was given to the technical requirements of high-end 3D printing. The model was designed with specific tolerancing for interchangeable parts, keying systems for assembly, and structural integrity for large-scale resin printing. The anatomy and surface textures were specifically pushed to ensure that fine details like skin pores, fabric weaves, and metallic scars would translate perfectly into the physical medium.
